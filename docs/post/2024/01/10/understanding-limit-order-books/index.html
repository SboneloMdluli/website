üìä Understanding Limit Order Books

January 10, 2024
trading markets microstructure

A limit order book is a fundamental concept in market microstructure that shows the quantity of securities being bid or offered at various price levels.

Key Components

  • Bid side: Orders to buy at specific prices
  • Ask side: Orders to sell at specific prices
  • Spread: Difference between best bid and ask prices

Market Impact

Understanding limit order books helps traders:

  • Gauge market depth and liquidity
  • Analyze price impact of trades
  • Develop effective execution strategies
